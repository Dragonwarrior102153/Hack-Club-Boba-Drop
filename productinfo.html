Company background

This company started as a dream of a 16-year old teenager with Dysgraphia. This disorder made it hard to write for him, but writing was a necessary part of high school . He thought, "What if I made a way for people like me to not have to wite so much, while actually understanding the content." He made this his goal in life and later on, he made his own patented Mega Pen that can explain and do your work for you! This product is perfect for everyone of all ages and gender, as writing is a common part of everyone's life that can be solved with this product.




The product

The product is a futuristic pen that can explain and do your work for you. It can do the work, and then explain it in way you can understand, on a seperate sheet of paper, or say it out loud as our product has state-of-the-art speakers built in. Our product is very cheap considering its materials and use, costing only $300.
